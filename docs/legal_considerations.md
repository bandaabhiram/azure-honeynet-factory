# Legal Considerations

## Is This Entrapment?

**No.** Entrapment requires law enforcement inducement to commit a crime. Honeypots are passive observational tools. You are not inducing anyone to attack — you are detecting attacks that would have occurred against real assets.

## Synthetic Data

All data in honeypots is computer-generated (Faker library). No real customer PII is stored.

## Jurisdiction

- Deploy in compliance with local laws
- Notify legal/ compliance team before deployment
- Do not log traffic from known trusted partners without consent
